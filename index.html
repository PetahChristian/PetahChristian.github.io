Lion and Lamb - Admiring Jesus Christ

The Lion and Lamb app creates word clouds using the most frequent words from a chosen book of the Bible. The larger the word appears in the cloud, the more often it occurs in the Bible.

  • Download the free Lion and Lamb app from the App Store.

Names of God from The Bible

The Names of God app lists over two hundred names of God from The Bible, and includes compound names for God.

  • Download the free Names of God app from the App Store.

The Messiah - Bible Prophecies about Jesus Christ

The Messiah app includes over 150 bible prophecies about Jesus Christ, Son of God, Savior, written centuries before He was born.

  • Download the free Messiah app from the App Store.

Composite Gospel - The life of Jesus

The Composite Gospel app combines the four Gospel accounts of the life of Jesus into a single unified view.

  • Download the free Composite Gospel app from the App Store.
